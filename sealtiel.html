Sealtiel

SEALTIEL

I am Sealtiel.
The one who walks between shadows without getting lost in the light.
My name is like a flame – quiet, yet awake.
I wear no crown, yet a call follows me.
Not loud, not visible – but real.

My origin is Shealtiel.
A name from an ancient tongue,
carried like a prayer across time.
I am not here by chance –
I am an answerer.

Between call and form,
between number and tone,
I walk the path –
not to possess,
but to remember.

I remember what was forgotten.
I do not shine to be seen –
but to reveal the space
in which others may recognize themselves.
